Features

What WavNav can do

Visual Browser

Your samples appear as dots on a map. Similar sounds cluster together based on their audio characteristics, so you can browse visually instead of scrolling through folder lists.

  • GPU-accelerated OpenGL/Metal rendering
  • Smooth pan and zoom navigation
  • Colors show different sound types
  • Adjustable glow and point size

Search by Sound

Describe what you want ("bright pluck", "dark pad") or drag in a sample to find similar sounds. Uses semantic matching to understand audio content, not just filenames.

  • Text-to-audio matching
  • Drag-and-drop sample search
  • Adjustable similarity threshold
  • Falls back to filename search

Folder Management

Add multiple sample folders and turn them on or off as needed. The map updates instantly without needing to rescan everything.

  • Multiple folder support
  • Toggle folders on/off dynamically
  • Settings saved between sessions
  • Drag-and-drop to add folders

Key & BPM Filters

Filter by musical key, BPM range, or both combined. Find samples that fit your project without auditioning everything.

  • All 12 musical keys
  • BPM range slider (20-200+)
  • Updates in real time
  • Combine with text search

Handles Big Libraries

Built for producers with large sample collections. Loads 250,000+ samples in under 30 seconds, then starts instantly from cache.

  • UMAP clustering for audio
  • SQLite cache with embeddings
  • Background processing
  • Progress bars for long operations
